Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Our Commitment
Welcome to VibeLink. We prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data to help you connect safely. By using VibeLink, you consent to these practices in full.
Who Can Use VibeLink
  • Age: You must be 18+ years old.
  • Safety: No felony or sex crime convictions allowed.
  • Compliance: You must follow all local and national laws.
Accounts of ineligible users will be terminated immediately.
Information We Collect
Data You Give Us:
  • Registration (Name, email, phone, DOB).
  • Profile (Gender, interests, bio).
  • Media (Photos and videos).
  • Messages sent within the app.
Data Collected Automatically:
  • Device model, OS, and IP address.
  • Approximate location (with your consent).
  • App usage and performance metrics.
Sensitive Permissions
To work properly, we request access to:
  • Geolocation: To show you nearby matches.
  • Camera/Gallery: To upload your best photos.
  • ML Kit: On-device face detection to ensure profile authenticity.
How Your Data is Used
We use your info to:
  • Create your account and match you with others.
  • Facilitate secure messaging.
  • Improve the app and process payments.
  • Keep the community safe from fraud and abuse.
Your Rights & Deletion
You stay in control:
  • Access: View your data at any time.
  • Delete: Use 'Delete Account' in Settings or the Web Portal.
  • Grace Period: Deletion takes 7 days to become permanent.
Security & Sharing
We use HTTPS/TLS encryption and secure cloud storage. We only share data with:
  • Service Providers: Google Firebase, RevenueCat, and ML Kit.
  • Legal: If required to protect user safety or comply with law.
We do not sell your personal data.
