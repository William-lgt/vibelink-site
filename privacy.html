Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Our Commitment
Welcome to VibeLink. We prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, process, and protect your info. By using VibeLink, you consent to these practices in full.
Who Can Use VibeLink
  • Age: You must be 18+ years old.
  • Safety: No felony or sex crime convictions.
  • Compliance: You must follow all applicable laws.
Accounts of ineligible users will be terminated immediately.
Information We Collect
Data You Give Us:
  • Registration (Name, email, phone, DOB).
  • Profile (Gender, interests, bio).
  • Media (Photos and videos).
  • Messages sent within the app.
Data Collected Automatically:
  • Device model, OS, and IP address.
  • Approximate location (with your consent).
  • App usage and performance metrics.
Sensitive Permissions
To work properly, we request access to:
  • Geolocation: To show you nearby matches.
  • Camera/Gallery: To upload your profile photos.
  • Face Detection: On-device detection to ensure authentic photos.
How Your Data is Used
We use your info to:
  • Create your account and match you with others.
  • Facilitate secure messaging.
  • Improve the app and process payments.
  • Keep the community safe and prevent fraud.
Your Rights & Deletion
You stay in control:
  • Access: View your data at any time.
  • Delete: Use 'Delete Account' in Settings or visit our Web Portal.
  • Grace Period: Permanent deletion takes 7 days.
